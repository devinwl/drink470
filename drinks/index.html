Monster Energy is an energy drink, launched by Monster Beverage Corporation in April 2002. The regular flavor comes in a black can with a green tear-shaped M logo, implied to have been torn by the claws of a monster. The company is also known for supporting many extreme sports events such as BMX, Motocross, skateboarding and snowboarding, as well as eSports.
